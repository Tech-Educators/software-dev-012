Images, Audio & Video

Images

img tags require the src attribute to tell it where to get the image

We can use external images in the src (hosted on somebody elses website)

Or we can download the images to our own project and use that

We can even do gifs!

The two benefits of using images your have saved are its quicker, and if the original owner takes their image down, you won't have it any more

They will work without an alt tag, but if you do that you're a bad person. Put alt tags on your image

Audio

Audio tags work a LOT like img tags, but, they are not self closing.

controls shows the controls, loop makes it loop, autoplay should be illegal don't do it (it also probably won't work on modern browsers)
